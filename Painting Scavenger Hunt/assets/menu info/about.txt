In 1865, shortly before the end of the Civil War, James Henry Beard painted The Night Before the Battle: an image which quickly gained notability for the familiarity those who had experienced the war felt with it.  But it has been a long time since the Civil War, and in this day and age, it may be more difficult for some of us to experience the true meaning of this masterpiece.  That is the goal of this project: to help bring the meaning of this painting into the modern age.
